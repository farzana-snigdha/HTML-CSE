Admission Requirements

The XXX in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) admission requirements will be as follows:

General Rules:
  • ​For admission to a course leading to the award of the Degree/Diploma of M.Sc. Engg./M.Engg./ PGD in different Engineering disciplines, a candidate must have obtained B.Sc. Engg./4-year B.Sc. Degree in the relevant branch or equivalent from any recognized institution, having a minimum CGPA of 3.0 out of 5.0 or 2.5 out of 4 in the relevant Bachelor’s programme and good performances in public examinations.
  • ​For admission to a course leading to M.Sc.(CSA) and PGD(CSA), a candidate must have Bachelor of Science degree in any field of engineering or 4-year B.Sc. Degree or its equivalent with sufficient background of Mathematics and Computer.​
  • ​Students for Masters programme can register on a full-time or a part-time basis and for PGD programme on Full-time basis only. Full-time students shall take a minimum of 9 credits and maximum of 12 credits per semester. Part-time students shall take a minimum of 6 credits and maximum of 9 credits per semester.​
  • Students for Ph.D. programme can register on a full-time or a part-time basis. Full-time students shall take a minimum of 9 credits and maximum of 12 credits per semester. Part-time students shall take a minimum of 6 credits and maximum of 9 credits per semester.​


Admission Procedure:
  • ​Application to the above courses shall be invited through regular and usual methods of announcement and shall be received by the Registrar. The normal admission time in M. Sc. Engg. and M. Sc. (CSA) programmes will remain the beginning of the Winter semester, as is usually announced in the admission brochure.
  • ​The final selection of a candidate will be made by the relevant PGC, in each department, which may also prescribe the required pre-requisites to be completed by the candidate before continuing the normal programme, if necessary.​
  • ​Every selected candidate shall pay the prescribed fees at the time of registration as per the current rules of the university.​
  • A candidate shall be provisionally selected by the postgraduate committee and may be required to pass the prerequisite non-credit courses as prescribed by the PGC.​


For more information please visit: https://admission.iutoic-dhaka.edu/
